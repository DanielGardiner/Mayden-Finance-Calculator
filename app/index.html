The Mayden Finance Calculator!

This is a simple finance calculator which will allow you to determine loan repayment details. Simply enter in three pieces of information relating to your loan below.

  • You may borrow between £1 and £8000
  • There is a 5% upfront admin fee applied to the amount borrowed
  • If borrowing above 80% (£6400) an additional £500 admin fee will apply
  • If borrowing above 90% (£7200) a further £500 admin fee will apply
  • You may choose your repayment as a percentage of your expected annual salary
